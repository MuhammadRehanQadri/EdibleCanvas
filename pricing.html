01. Researching

First step for any order is learning about the theme. We want to get every detail right for our clients. To ensure this we take the time to be knowledgable on all themes we commit to do.

02. Create Designs

We then create or select designs appropriate to the theme. This can include, selecting shapes that pair well together, creating a custom color palette, selecting fonts that tie in with the aesthetic of the set and theme, and ordering cutters, or hand cutting if needed.

03.Preparing the Canvas

The reason custom cookies are an art form like no other is because every part is created from scratch every single time. From the medium to decorate with to the canvas that is being decorated on. We select the freshest and most premium ingredients for every batch and then prepare dough, cut shapes and bake them.

04. Preparing The Medium- Royal Icing

Fresh royal icing is whipped together using premium vanilla, sifted powder sugar and meringue powder. Colors are chosen and developed to produce the right tones and hues that match our clients theme or event.

05. Decorate

Finally, we can take our research, our freshly prepared canvas and our medium to create magic. Each piece of edible art is handcrafted and hand detailed to perfection.

06.Package

Lastly, we package. To us, packaging is as important as any step. Each cookie is carefully bagged and heat sealed individually for freshness. Cookies are then arranged in a box tied with a beautiful raw silk ribbon and ready to be enjoyed.

 

the Pricing

 

All cookies are 3.5-4” in size

All our cookies come baked with premium ingredients and we use real vanilla beans to flavor our signature cookies.

All cookies come individually heat sealed which allows them to stay fresh at room temperature for up to 2-3weeks and be frozen up to 3 months.

For pricing we factor in the time it takes to complete a given set, ingredients, materials and packaging.

Custom cookie sets begin at $60/dozen.

A basic dozen can take anywhere from 6-12 hours from designing the concept, baking, making icing colors, different consistencies, cleaning up, decorating and packaging.
